About Me

Hello! My name is Peter, and I’m a passionate game developer from Canada. I hold degrees in Game Programming from Humber College and Information Technology from York University, which together provide me with a solid foundation in both the technical and creative aspects of game development.

In this portfolio, you’ll find a collection of my personal projects that showcase my skills in game design, programming, and 3D modeling. I specialize in using the Unity game engine and programming in C#, where I’ve developed a range of gameplay mechanics and interactive experiences. Additionally, I have hands-on experience in 3D modeling with Blender and 3Ds Max, enabling me to create detailed assets for my games.

Currently, I’m working on an ambitious project: developing my own game engine in C++. This endeavor is challenging my technical expertise and deepening my understanding of the intricacies of game development.
